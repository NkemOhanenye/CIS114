Welcome

Moe in anime has been around for a long period of time. This is a site dedicated to explain the effects that moe has had on the state of the anime community, from the creators to the fans. But first, what is moe?

Moe is a term coined by anime fans in Japan in the 80s, that refers to the strong feelings of affection toward a character (usually female) in anime, manga, video games, etc. The word comes from the Japanese word "Moeru (萌える)" which means "to be infactuated".

Some of you may be wondering as to why the dates start from the 70s; the reason of this decision lies in that while "modern anime" started back in the 60s with Tezuka Osamu's Tetsuwan no Atom (Astro Boy), the aspects that really made moe come to be began in the 70s, so there was no reason to bring up the discussion before then.
